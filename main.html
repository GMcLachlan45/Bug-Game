Buggu Game

How to Play

Once loaded, the game will begin automatically. The goal is to prevent any two cells from reaching a 30 degree arc somewhere on the edge of the petri dish.
To do this you have 20 drops of poison. Click anywhere within the dish to drop poison. Once dropped, the poison will grow outward, killing any cells it comes in contact with.
You win the game if you kill every cell. You lose the game if any 2 cells reach their goal. Good luck!
