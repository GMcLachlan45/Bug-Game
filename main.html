How to Play

Once loaded, the game will begin automatically. The goal is to prevent any two cells from growing to a radius of 30 degrees.
To do this you have drops of poison. Click anywhere on the sphere to drop poison. Once dropped, the poison will grow outward, killing any cells it comes in contact with.
You win the game if you kill every cell. You lose the game if any 2 cells reach a radius of 30 degrees. Good luck!
